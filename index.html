Who I am

I'm an Operations and IT Project Manager who has spent the last few years building systems and delivering outcomes that actually work, not just on paper, but in the day-to-day reality of fast-moving teams.

My career has moved from establishing operational foundations from scratch to formally training in IT Project Management, where I stepped into a live product environment and led delivery from the ground up.
