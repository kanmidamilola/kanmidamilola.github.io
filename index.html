02

How I Manage Projects

01

Clarify the Outcome

Define scope, timeline, and success metrics before a single task is assigned. Ambiguity at the start becomes a crisis at the end.

02

Structure the Work

Break projects into clear deliverables and assign ownership. Everyone should know what they own and when it's due.

03

Align the Team

Establish communication cadence and shared visibility. A team that knows what's happening doesn't need to be chased.

04

Maintain Momentum

Resolve blockers, manage dependencies, and keep stakeholders informed. Delivery momentum is protected, not assumed.
